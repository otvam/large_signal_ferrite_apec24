EPCOS TDK N87 - Large-Signal Permitivitty
=========================================

Measured with a I14/1.5/5 core.
Measured at a temperature of 25 C.

Dataset source:
    - Characterization and Impact of Large-Signal Dielectric Properties in MnZn Ferrites
    - Thomas Guillod, William V. R. Roberts, and Charles R. Sullivan
    - IEEE APEC 2024

Variable description:
    - f_vec - frequency vector in Hz (size n_f)
    - E_mat - peak electric field matrix (size n_f x n_E)
    - eps_mat - real permitivitty matrix (size n_f x n_E)
    - sigma_mat - real conductivity matrix (size n_f x n_E)
