EPCOS TDK N87 - Small-Signal Permeability
=========================================

Measured with a R34 core.
Measured at a temperature of 25 C.
The peak flux density is maintained below 0.25 mT.

Dataset source:
    - SIFERRIT Material N87, Datasheet
    - TDK / EPCOS, 2021
    - https://tdk-electronics.tdk.com

Variable description:
    - f_vec - frequency vector in Hz (size n_f)
    - mu_vec - complex permeability matrix (size n_f)
